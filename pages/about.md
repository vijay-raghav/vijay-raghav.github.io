# **About Me**

I started my career as a software engineer, but I quickly realized my true passion wasn't just writing the code—it was defining *what* we build, *why* we build it, and *who* we build it for. Today, I am an AI-native product strategist pursuing my Tech MBA (Master of Science in Product Management) at Carnegie Mellon University's Tepper School of Business. 

I am obsessed with one question: **How can AI transform the way we build and deliver value?**

With a deep technical foundation in computer engineering and artificial intelligence, I've spent much of the past year identifying tangible use cases for agentic automation and large-scale AI in the enterprise. My focus is entirely on bridging the gap between industry hype and ROI-positive product implementations. From building RAG-based systems to optimizing LLM evaluations and multi-agent workflows, I am passionate about transitioning product lifecycles from manual oversight to highly reliable agentic systems.

While I love building intelligent platforms, my core philosophy always comes back to deep user empathy and scaled execution. Previously, I led the 0-to-1 development of the **US Open** digital platform, guiding 50+ member cross-functional teams and scaling a #1 industry-ranked fan experience to over 500k+ users. I've also leveraged this enterprise leadership experience to solve complex operational problems, including a massive supply chain consolidation for a Fortune 50 tech giant that saved \$500k annually.

### Core Competencies

*   **AI Product Development:** Generative & Agentic AI, RAG Architectures, LLM Evals, & Multi-Agent Systems.
*   **UX & Product Discovery:** User & Market Research, Design Sprints, Customer Journey Mapping, & Digital Customer Experience.
*   **End-to-End Execution:** 0-to-1 PLM, Strategic Roadmapping (RICE/MoSCoW), GTM Strategy, & Agile/Scrum Leadership.
*   **Technical & Data Stack:** REST API Integrations, Cloud Platforms, Python, SQL, & High-Scale Architecture.

When I step away from my laptop, you can usually find me at a local pub trivia night quizzing, hiking a new trail, or out on a motorcycle ride.
